Marquee Albums



Started in February 2022, The Time-Warp is an exciting audio experience that combines the best of live radio and podcasting to present listeners with an informative whirlwind nostalgia trip. Each episode looks at one specific year in music and explores the best songs and albums both critically and commercially and includes several recurring segments such as the Marquee Album, where I pick either the most important or my favourite album from the year and play 3 songs that showcase what sets it apart and makes it such an essential listen, as well as a segment where a song from the chosen year is linked to the output of an artist born in the year.
